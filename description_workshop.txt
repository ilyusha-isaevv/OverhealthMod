[b]Overhealth[/b] is a mod that introduces a temporary overhealing mechanic.

[h2]How it works[/h2]
When you receive healing while already at maximum health, any excess healing is converted into temporary [b]Overhealth[/b]:
[list]
[*] Works as extension of max life.
[*] Overhealth slowly decays over time. The more Overhealth you have, the faster it decays.
[*] Supports all healing methods, except natural health regen.
[*] Works in multiplayer.
[/list]

[h2]Mod Configuration[/h2]
In [b]Gameplay Config[/b] you can configure the maximum overhealth amount and decay rates. Check it out to balance the experience for your own gameplay!
In [b]Visuals Config[/b] you can configure the overhealth UI.

[h2]Cross-Mod Compatibility[/h2]
