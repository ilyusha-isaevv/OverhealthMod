[b]Overhealth[/b] is a mod that introduces a temporary overhealing mechanic.

[h2]How it works[/h2]
When you receive healing while already at maximum health, any excess healing is converted into temporary [b]Overhealth[/b]. Overhealth slowly decays over time.
This mechanic supports various healing methods, including:
[list]
    [*] Healing potions
    [*] Collecting hearts from the ground
    [*] Spectre Armor set bonus healing and etc.
[/list]
[i]This mod works in multiplayer.[/i]

[h2]UI Support[/h2]
The mod displays your health over your maximum health (e.g., [b]520/400 HP[/b]).
It supports all vanilla health display sets (Classic, Fancy, and Horizontal Bars).

[h2]Mod Configuration[/h2]
Gameplayer config contains (it's recommended to keep it at default values):
[list]
    [*] Decay Rate Mutiplier - Overall decay rate multiplier.
    [*] Constant decay rate - Constant decay rate of overhealth as a percentage of max life per second.
    [*] Progressive decay rate - Additional decay based on current overhealth amount.
[/list]

[h2]Cross-Mod Compatibility[/h2]
At the moment of release (v1.0), this mod does not have cross-mod compatibility, meaning:
[list]
    [*] other mods might bypass or break the overhealth mechanic (e.g. reset overhealth to 0).
    [*] custom healing methods introduced by other mods will not transfer excess healing into overhealth.
[/list]
[b]Official compatibility for other popular mods will be added in future updates.[/b]
