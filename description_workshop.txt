[b]Overhealth[/b] is a mod that introduces a temporary overhealing mechanic.

[h2]How it works[/h2]
When you receive healing while already at maximum health, any excess healing is converted into temporary [b]Overhealth[/b]. Overhealth slowly decays over time.
This mechanic supports almost all healing methods (except natural health regen, which is capped at max health).
[i]Also this mod works in multiplayer![/i]

[h2]Mod Configuration[/h2]
The config contains settings mostly for decay rates. Check it out to balance the experience for your own gameplay!

[h2]Cross-Mod Compatibility[/h2]
Unsupported mods might break the overhealth mechanic (e.g. reset overhealth to 0).
