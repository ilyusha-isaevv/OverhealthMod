Overhealth is a mod that introduces a temporary overhealing mechanic.

How it works:
When you receive healing while already at maximum health, any excess healing is converted into temporary Overhealth:
- Works as extension of max life.
- Overhealth slowly decays over time. The more Overhealth you have, the faster it decays.
- Supports all healing methods, except natural health regen.
- Works in multiplayer.

Mod Configuration:
In Gameplay Config you can configure the maximum overhealth amount and decay rates. Check it out to balance the experience for your own gameplay!
In Visuals Config you can configure the overhealth UI.

Cross-Mod Compatibility:
If you noticed that suddenly all your overhealth resets, it means that some mod is unsupported, which breaks the mechanic.
To check out which mods are supported read the mod page on Steam Workshop.

Official compatibility for other mods will be added in future updates if requested.
