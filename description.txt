Overhealth is a mod that introduces a temporary overhealing mechanic.

How it works:
When you receive healing while already at maximum health, any excess healing is converted into temporary Overhealth. Overhealth slowly decays over time.
This mechanic supports almost all healing methods (except natural health regen, which is capped at max health).
Also this mod works in multiplayer!

Mod Configuration:
The config contains settings mostly for decay rates. Check it out to balance the experience for your own gameplay!

Cross-Mod Compatibility:
Unsupported mods might break the overhealth mechanic (e.g. reset overhealth to 0).
To check out which mods are supported read the mod page on Steam Workshop.

Official compatibility for other popular mods will be added in future updates.
